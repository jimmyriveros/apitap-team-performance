Average time
Mean duration per ticket. Calculated from when the person received it until reassigned, resolved, closed, or until Jul 2, 2026 if still open. Format: m=months, d=days, h=hours.

How many times on average a ticket was assigned to this person. 1.00 means no re-assignments. Values >1 indicate tickets that bounced back to them more than once.
